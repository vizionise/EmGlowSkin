Skin & Treatments

An esthetician is a licensed skincare professional who performs treatments such as facials, waxing, brow and lash services, and product guidance to support healthy skin.

EmGlowSkin offers customized facials, peels, microdermabrasion, dermaplaning, waxing, brow services, lash lifts, and beauty add-ons.

Most clients benefit from a facial every 4 to 6 weeks, though your ideal rhythm depends on your skin goals, lifestyle, and treatment plan.

A gentle, customized facial is best for sensitive skin. Emily will review your skin, products, and sensitivities before choosing exfoliation, masks, and finishing products.

Facials should feel comfortable and relaxing. Some extractions or active treatments may feel mildly tingly, but your comfort is always considered.

Many clients notice a glow after one treatment, while concerns like acne, texture, or pigmentation usually improve with a consistent series and home care.
