About EmGlowSkin

Meet Your Esthetician

Hi! I'm Emily Keith, a licensed Esthetician with a passion for helping people feel confident and radiant in their own skin. My journey into skincare started with my own struggles, discovering how the right treatments could transform not just my skin, but my confidence. That's when I knew I wanted to help others experience the same.

The Story Behind EmGlowSkin

EmGlowSkin is more than just a business, it's my dream come to life. After completing my training and working in a med spa environment, I had the opportunity to learn from skilled professionals and gain hands-on experience with a wide range of treatments. Now, I've created a space of my own where every service is built around you, your skin, your goals, and your unique beauty.

My Philosophy

Skincare isn't one-size-fits-all. I believe in creating customized experiences that leave you glowing inside and out. Every product, every technique, and every moment in my studio is centered around making you feel like your best self.
