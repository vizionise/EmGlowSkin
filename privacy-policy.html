Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EmGlowSkin collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website, contact us, or book services. This page is a simple informational policy and is not legal advice.

Information We May Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, appointment request, message details, service interests, and communication preferences.

When you book an appointment through our online booking link, your booking and payment information may be collected by Square or another third-party booking provider. Their privacy policy and terms may also apply.

During consultation or service, EmGlowSkin may ask for skincare concerns, allergies, product use, recent treatments, medications, sensitivities, or other information needed to help determine whether a service is appropriate for you.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to questions, schedule appointments, provide services, send appointment-related updates, improve the website, maintain client records, and communicate about services or promotions if you choose to receive them.

Cookies and Website Data

This website may use cookies, basic analytics, or similar tools to understand website traffic and improve the visitor experience. You can usually adjust cookie settings in your browser.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, booking, payment processing, email, analytics, maps, social links, or spam prevention. We may also share information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or with your consent.

Third-Party Links

This website links to Square, Google, Facebook, Instagram, Google Maps, and other third-party services. EmGlowSkin is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable care to protect information, but no website or online transmission is guaranteed to be fully secure. We keep information only as long as needed for business, scheduling, service, recordkeeping, or legal purposes.

Your Choices

You may contact us to ask about your information, request updates, request deletion where appropriate, or opt out of promotional messages. Some information may need to be retained for legitimate business, legal, tax, or safety reasons.

Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13. Minors should use this website and book services only with involvement from a parent or guardian.
